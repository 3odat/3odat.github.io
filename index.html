About Me

I am an innovative cybersecurity professional and PhD researcher working at the intersection of AI, security, and autonomous robotics. My current work focuses on securing UAV ecosystems end‑to‑end: from PX4 autopilot layers and MAVLink communications to AI‑assisted mission planning and multi‑agent collaboration using LangGraph and large language models.
